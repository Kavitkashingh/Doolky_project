About Us

EazyDiner has been created by a team that is extremely passionate about food, wine and all things fine. As a collective group of Chefs and Hoteliers who have worked in some of the best hotels and restaurants around the world, we were always on the lookout for new dining experiences and exciting cuisines. It is this love and passion for eating and exploring that brought us together to create EazyDiner.

How often do you find yourself wondering, where you should go eat on a special occasion? What are the newest and top eateries in town? What are the things you absolutely must try when visiting a particular restaurant? Can you save some money while still dining at top restaurants of the city?

EazyDiner has all the answers for the most enjoyable, authentic and friction-free table booking experience. With an instant confirmation of less than 18 seconds, you can now book over 15,000+ restaurants in Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Goa, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Hyderabad, Chandigarh and over 150 cities in India & Dubai on our swanky website or mobile app. For bookings in India, we also provide a 24 x 7 complimentary concierge service.

With every meal you book through EazyDiner, there comes a phenomenal deal ranging from free alcoholic drinks to up to 50% off on your total bill. With no membership fees or reservations charge, what is best is that these eye-popping deals are not for just one person, but are applicable on the entire table! Now, this is what I call a real deal!

EazyDiner is evolving each day to become your daily dining app by providing pre-paid discounted meals and vouchers, including upto 50% off at Chaayos, Burger King and many others! If we had our way, these prepaid meals would be the only gifts our families would be giving us!

With exclusive and expert reviews by top critics helmed by our co-founder, the iconic Vir Sanghvi, EazyDiner serves as a curated guide to an extensive range of dining options and is your reliable guide to eating out.

With each booking, you also earn EazyPoints, which can be redeemed for an array of delicious treats. You earn points for booking through EazyDiner as well as by leaving user reviews on restaurant profiles. The more points you earn, the more delicious and exciting the rewards become!

Do take the time to #ThankTheStars - an initiative by EazyDiner which allows you to acknowledge the efforts of your servers and chefs at the restaurant and help them win amazing rewards while you get 200 EazyPoints for every review. It’s a total win-win.

If you're looking for large group bookings of more than 10 guests, suggestions, ideas or recommendations on eating out; just call our 24 hour EazyConcierge at 786 100 4444, all 7 days a week!

Take the Eazy way out and become an Eazy Diner for life!

FAQs:

1. What is EazyDiner Prime? Ans: EazyDiner Prime promises exclusive access to the hottest bars and restaurants and guarantees you a minimum 25%, and up to 50% off at top restaurants in India & Dubai. Select a suitable membership plan (1-Year/3-Months/1-Month) and get guaranteed discounts at 2000+ premium restaurants along with VIP access across India and UAE.

2. How do I pay with EazyDiner? Ans: Book a table at a PayEazy-enabled restaurant from the EazyDiner App. Finish your meal & open the app. Click Pay Bill on the ‘Upcoming Booking’ section from the home screen. Enter your bill amount and choose from your favourite payment mode to pay.

3. How do I get free EazyDiner Prime? Ans: EazyDiner Prime is a paid-only membership. Keep checking the EazyDiner app for latest offers and limited-time FREE memberships.

4. How do I cancel my EazyDiner Prime membership? Ans: You can initiate cancellation of your EazyDiner Prime membership anytime; however, the membership will be active till the subscription period, post which it will remain cancelled. Raise a request at prime@eazydiner.com.

5. How do I upload my bill to EazyDiner? Ans: Once you’ve dined through EazyDiner, you will get an option to ‘Upload your Bill’. Follow the steps and just make sure that the bill images are clear along with the proper bill number, restaurant name, date, time and amount. Handwritten bills or manual entries will not be accepted.

6. How do I redeem EazyDiner points? Ans: Earn EazyPoints with every reservation and redeem them for Prime memberships, a complimentary meal for two or a hotel stay! All redemption requests are to be sent to booking@eazydiner.com.

7. What is Payeazy? Ans: PayEazy is a payment channel provider that helps you pay the restaurant bill. Go cashless and pay the restaurant bill via PayEazy, which helps you make a transaction with any debit or credit card and other popular payment gateways and enjoy offers and discounts from our payment partners.

8. How do I get additional discounts using PayEazy? Ans: Payeazy lets you save extra over and above the restaurant deal. You can pay on the EazyDiner app using your favourite payment option and choose from an exhaustive list of banking partners.

9. How do I cancel an EazyDiner booking? Ans: You can cancel your booking anytime in your profile section through the app, website or simply by calling the EazyConcierge at 786 100 4444 (valid only for customers bookings in India). EazyDiner has no cancellation charges.

10. How do I use EazyDiner? Ans: You can use EazyDiner by visiting www.eazydiner.com or download its mobile app on Android/iOS devices.

11. What restaurants have the best deals? Ans: Restaurants listed on EazyDiner provide you with top-notch deals. Get minimum guaranteed 25% up to 50% OFF at EazyDiner Prime-enabled restaurants
